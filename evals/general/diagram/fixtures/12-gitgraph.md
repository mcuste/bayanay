Draw the git workflow for our team's release process. We have a main branch and a develop branch. Two feature branches: feature/oauth2 and feature/stripe-payments both branch off develop. feature/oauth2 is completed first and merges back to develop. Then feature/stripe-payments merges to develop. We do a release: develop merges to main, tagged as v2.0.0. Post-release, a hotfix branch is cut from main to fix a critical billing calculation bug. Hotfix commits the fix, then merges to both main (tagged v2.0.1) and back to develop. Then a new feature/dark-mode branches from develop with one commit.
