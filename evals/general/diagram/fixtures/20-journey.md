Map the user journey for purchasing a SaaS subscription. Sections and tasks: Discovery section — Find product via Google search (satisfaction 3, actor: Prospect), Read landing page (4, Prospect), Watch demo video (4, Prospect), Check pricing page (3, Prospect). Trial section — Sign up for free trial (4, User), Receive onboarding email (3, User), Complete setup wizard (3, User), First meaningful feature use (5, User). Evaluation section — Hit trial limitation (2, User), Compare plan tiers (3, User), Talk to sales rep (4, User, Sales). Conversion section — Enter payment details (2, User), Confirm subscription (5, User), Receive confirmation email (4, User). Retention section — Weekly active usage (5, User), Share with teammate (5, User, Colleague), Annual renewal (4, User).
