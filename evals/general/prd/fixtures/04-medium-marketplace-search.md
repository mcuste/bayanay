Draft a PRD for overhauling search on our freelancer marketplace. We have 50k freelancer profiles and 10k active job postings. Current search is basic keyword matching on titles only — users complain results are irrelevant and they can't filter by availability, hourly rate, or timezone. We see 60% of users abandon search after the first page. Both clients hiring freelancers and freelancers looking for gigs use search, but their needs are quite different.
