# Clarification Needed: "AI Thing for Our App"

This request doesn't have enough context to draft a meaningful PRD. Before writing anything, I need to understand the fundamentals. Here are the questions I'd need answered:

**Problem & Users**
- What problem are your users experiencing today that you believe AI could solve? What are they doing instead right now (workarounds, manual processes, competitor tools)?
- Who are your users? What does your app do, and what domain is it in?
- What evidence do you have that this is a real pain point — support tickets, churn data, user feedback, lost deals?

**Scope & Constraints**
- When you say "AI thing," what user-facing behavior are you imagining? (e.g., generating content, answering questions, classifying data, making recommendations, automating a workflow)
- What data do you already have that an AI feature could work with? Are there privacy or compliance constraints on that data?
- Is this a new capability or an enhancement to something that already exists in the app?

**Goals**
- What does success look like in 6 months? How would you know this feature is working?
- Is there competitive pressure driving this, or is it driven by user demand?

Without answers to these questions, a PRD would be fiction — I'd be inventing the problem, the users, and the scope. A good PRD captures real product decisions, not hypothetical ones.

Once you can describe the problem your users have and what behavior they'd see, I can draft a focused PRD.
