Draft an RFC for switching our ORM from SQLAlchemy to raw SQL queries using asyncpg. Our current ADR-003 says "Use SQLAlchemy for all database access to maintain consistency and reduce SQL injection risk." But we're hitting performance bottlenecks in our analytics pipeline — queries that take 2ms in raw SQL take 50-80ms through SQLAlchemy due to ORM overhead, object hydration, and the synchronous driver. The analytics endpoints serve dashboards that aggregate millions of rows and our P95 latency is 4 seconds when it should be under 500ms. This targets implementation and ADR.
